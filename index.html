Kenco Aviation - Ian Hanmore

Overview

Kenco Aviation is the online persona of Ian Hanmore, a 25-year-old flight simulation content creator from Kent, England. He started with Flight Simulator 2004 at age 7 and now streams flights using Microsoft Flight Simulator, X-Plane, and Prepar3D. The name "Kenco" comes from his father, "KencoKev," who loved Kenco coffee.
